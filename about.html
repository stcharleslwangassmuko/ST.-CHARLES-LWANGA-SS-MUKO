About Our School

St. Charles Lwanga S.S Muko is a mixed day and boarding secondary school located in Rubanda District. The school provides quality education with strong Christian values, fostering discipline, excellence, and leadership.

History

Founded in 1995, St. Charles Lwanga Secondary School, Muko has been dedicated to providing quality education rooted in discipline, faith, and academic excellence.

School Motto

Intelligence Aims at Wise Action

School Mission

To provide quality, inclusive, and holistic education through effective teaching, moral guidance, and practical learning experiences, empowering learners to achieve academic excellence and contribute positively to society.

School Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, nurturing disciplined, innovative, and responsible citizens equipped with knowledge, skills, and values for a dynamic world.
